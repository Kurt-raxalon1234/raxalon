How long will my project take?

The timeline for your project will depend on the size and scope of the work. We will provide you with a detailed schedule once we have a better understanding of your project.

Do you have insurance?

Yes, we carry comprehensive insurance coverage to protect both our company and our clients. This insurance typically includes general liability insurance, worker's compensation insurance, and commercial auto insurance. General liability insurance protects us from claims of property damage or personal injury that may arise during the course of construction. Worker's compensation insurance provides benefits to our employees in the event they are injured on the job. Commercial auto insurance covers our vehicles in case of an accident.

What permits do I need for my project?

The permitting requirements will vary depending on your project and local regulations. We can help you determine what permits are needed and guide you through the permitting process.

Who will I be working with on my project?

You will have a dedicated project manager who will be your main point of contact throughout the project. This project manager will oversee the day-to-day operations of your project, ensuring that it is completed on time, on budget, and to your specifications. They will be responsible for communicating with you regularly to keep you updated on the progress of your project, addressing any questions or concerns you may have, and working with our team of skilled construction professionals to ensure that your project is completed to the highest standards.

What happens if there are unforeseen changes to the project?

We will work with you to address any unforeseen changes that may arise during construction. Any changes will be documented in a change order that must be approved by you before the work proceeds.
